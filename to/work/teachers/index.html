How it Works

  1. Browse the Options Catalog and click Add to collect building blocks.
  2. Open New Plan ‚Üí give it a title, country, short story, needs, and attach selected options.
  3. Add your funding links (Interac, BTC, ETH, SOL, Patreon, OpenCollective, etc.).
  4. Click Publish (Local) to generate a sharable page URL (hash-based) from your browser.
  5. Export your data to JSON for safekeeping or to migrate elsewhere.
Funding safety & transparency tips
  • Prefer routes you control (Interac e-Transfer, your wallet addresses).
  • Clearly state what funds do (e.g., ‚Äú$10 ‚Üí print packs for 20 students‚Äù).
  • Share periodic updates (photos, receipts) in your plan story.
  • Add a small independent peer review (a colleague can verify receipts).
