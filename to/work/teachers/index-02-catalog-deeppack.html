Notes on “near-zero cost” language
  • Items labeled “$0” are typically free tiers or open platforms with no required payment.
  • Items labeled “near-$0” may have optional paid features, but are usable without payment for small classes.
  • Self-host options are listed only when a reputable free hosted alternative exists or setup is trivial.
